About

Our university have many group projects. However some students are faced with difficulties finding team members when it is required for them to execute projects in a team, Some teams may have incomplete members, and some teams end up having a member that may not contribute much towards the success of the project due to lack of required skills

Our website aims to link teams requiring member(s) and students requiring teams togetherm. A student can make a request for members to complete his or her team and also can respond to an existing request for team members, indicating interest in joining the team
